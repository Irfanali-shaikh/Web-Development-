Welcome to KCA ST. FRANCIS OF ASSISI INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL


The School is run by the Kerala Catholic Association(KCA)regd. way back in 1959. Ever since its inception,KCA was engaged in many social and charitable works.
The visionaries had put on their thinking caps to start an institution of international level to enable the unreachable to conquer the quality education . With this in view , the KCA Started the school named KCA ST. FRANCIS OF ASSISI INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL, in June 2011 with a strength of 30 students beginning with play school and Nursery.
